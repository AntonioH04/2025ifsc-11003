university of arkansas at little rock: a brief history

overview

the university of arkansas at little rock (ua little rock) is a public research university in little rock, arkansas. established as little rock junior college in 1927, it became little rock university in 1957 and joined the university of arkansas system in 1969.

academics

ua little rock offers more than 100 undergraduate and 60 graduate degrees across three colleges and a law school. an inline quote example: education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.
